  ---
  Complete Configuration Reference

  Types imply ranges unless an explicit constraint narrows them:
  u8: 0–255 · u16: 0–65,535 · u32: 0–4,294,967,295 · u64: 0–18,446,744,073,709,551,615 · usize: 0–2^64−1 · i32/i64:
  signed · f64 jitter: typed 0.0–∞ but semantically 0.0–1.0 · bool: true/false · Duration: 0s–∞ · Option<T>: None or T's
   range

  7 · PostgreSQL (rsfga-storage/src/postgres.rs → PostgresConfig)

  Field: database_url

  Default: "postgres://localhost/rsfga"

  Field: max_connections

  Type: Option<u64>

  Field: write_timeout_secs
